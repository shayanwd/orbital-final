Founded in January 2024 by Italian DJ and producer Alignament, Orbital was born with the mission of redefining musical creation, breaking down every barrier between sound and human perception. More than just a label, it is a sonic movement designed to push the limits of hard techno, transforming music into an immersive experience that transcends reality.

Through deep, hypnotic rhythms and relentless energy, Orbital invites listeners to lose themselves in the vastness of the universe, where sound becomes the only guide in an infinite journey beyond time and space.
