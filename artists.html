Alignment

Alignment

The Italian wonder boy has evolved into a powerhouse of modern techno. From his early days in Berlin's underground scene, Alignment has ascended to dominate the biggest stages across Europe and beyond.

Known for his signature blend of hard techno and 90s-inspired trance, his music is defined by powerful melodies, deep basslines, driving kicks, and ethereal, echoing synths. Alignment continues to shape the future of techno, cementing his place as one of the genre's most exciting and visionary artists.

Anxhela

Anxhela

Italian-Albanian DJ Anxhela, now based in Lisbon, has been immersed in electronic music since adolescence. She embraced techno at 17, later moving to Berlin, where she began DJing in 2020.

Her first releases came in 2022 on MRC and Voxnox, leading up to her 2024 EP Black Hole on Orbital Records. Blending hard techno with trance and acid, her high-energy sets are marked by power and intensity, making her a rising force in the global scene.
